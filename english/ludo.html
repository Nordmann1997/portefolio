LUDO

Ludo is a board game created under the design office at OsloMet Product Design. In short, it is a group of students from all grades who solve a problem for an external client. This Ludo game is designed to be aesthetically pleasing when the game is stored and works just as well as a decorative object as it does as a game. The idea behind the game was to create a game that could be part of the interior of a home, without having to be hidden in a drawer or cupboard.

Construction process briefly summarized

Book box

The box that represents a book is made of oak by a skilled cabinetmaker who was part of our group. It is constructed in solid oak and is later painted with decoration.

Engraving

The game illustration is engraved with a laser cutter to create a lasting finish in the oak board. The board is then covered with tape before another round of laser engraving, so that the game can be precisely colored in the right areas.

Decoration

Here you can see the board after it has been laser cut with tape on. After this, the different fields are colored with a brush and paint. When the tape is then removed, the paint remains with a perfect finish.

MAGNET HOLDER

The magnet speaker is a project where I have experimented with magnetic materials to create a unique sound experience. The speaker is made from recycled materials and has a minimalist design that fits into any home.

The Building Process Summarized

Material Selection

The materials used in the speaker are carefully selected to provide the best sound quality and an aesthetically pleasing appearance. The main material is recycled wood combined with magnetic components.

Assembly

The assembly process involves careful placement of the magnets to ensure optimal sound quality. The video shows how the components are put together.
